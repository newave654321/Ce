# Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the intended evolution of the Ce protocol.

It is directional, not contractual.

---

## Phase 1 — Protocol Stabilization

* Definitions finalized
* Core concepts clarified
* Structural consistency review

---

## Phase 2 — Reference Architectures

* Logical models
* Node interaction patterns
* Recall structures

---

## Phase 3 — Experimental Implementations

* Conceptual implementations
* Software and hardware proofs of concept

---

## Phase 4 — Applied Domains

* Memory systems
* Distributed data systems
* Energy and infrastructure contexts

Progress is determined by agreement and coherence, not timelines.
