# Hardware Research

This document explores potential hardware expressions of Ce-aligned systems.
It is speculative and non-normative.

---

## Areas of Inquiry

### Sensor-Oriented Systems

Investigating hardware that observes persistent signals.

Examples:

* Environmental sensors
* Energy flow monitors
* Participation density indicators

---

### Edge Presence

Exploring distributed, low-power hardware that observes without commanding.

Considerations:

* Local processing
* Minimal data retention
* Observability without surveillance

---

### Physical Recall

Studying how memory may be embedded physically without enforcement.

Examples:

* Passive logs
* Slow indicators
* Degradation-aware memory

---

Hardware research does not imply required devices or architectures.
