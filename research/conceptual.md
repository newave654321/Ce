# Conceptual Research

This document explores conceptual extensions of Ce beyond the core protocol.
It is speculative and non-normative.

---

## Areas of Inquiry

### Coherence as Infrastructure

Investigating coherence as a structural property rather than a behavioral rule.

Questions:

* Can coherence be observed without instrumentation?
* How does coherence persist across scale?

---

### Time-Scale Awareness

Exploring slow-time signals (e.g., carbon, energy, trust) versus fast control loops.

Questions:

* What breaks when systems collapse slow signals into commands?
* How can recall remain informative without becoming prescriptive?

---

### Agreement Without Enforcement

Studying agreement as alignment rather than obligation.

Questions:

* What replaces compliance in long-lived systems?
* How does exit function as a stabilizing force?

---

This document exists to surface questions, not answers.
