# Agreement

Agreement is the mechanism by which shared states are recognized.

---

## Formation of Agreement

Agreement forms when participating nodes voluntarily recognize a state as valid.

Agreement does not require unanimity.
It requires sufficient recognition to establish coherence.

---

## Persistence of Agreement

Agreement persists while:

* Recognition remains voluntary
* Coherence is maintained
* No coercive dependency is introduced

---

## Withdrawal of Agreement

Nodes may withdraw recognition at any time.
Withdrawal must not invalidate prior recall.

---

## Non-Agreement

Disagreement does not imply failure.
Non-alignment is permissible provided coherence is preserved.
