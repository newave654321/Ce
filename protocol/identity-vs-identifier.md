# Identity vs Identifier

The Ce protocol distinguishes identity from identifier.

---

## Identifier

An identifier is a functional reference.
It enables distinction without assertion of authority or ownership.

---

## Identity

Identity is contextual, external, and non-normative.
The protocol does not depend on identity.

---

## Constraint

Any implementation in which identity alters agreement, coherence, or recall is non-compliant.

---

This separation preserves neutrality and prevents capture.
