# Recall

Recall enables reconstruction of recognized states.

---

## Conditions for Recall

Recall is valid when:

* Prior agreement existed
* Coherence has not been violated
* Reconstruction does not rely on authority

---

## Distributed Recall

Recall may be distributed across nodes.
No single node is required to possess complete memory.

---

## Recall Integrity

Recall must:

* Preserve original meaning of the state
* Avoid reinterpretation through identity
* Remain verifiable through agreement records

---

Recall is a property of the system, not of any node.
