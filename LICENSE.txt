## Ce Commercial Use Policy

### (Coherence-Bound Use)

Notwithstanding the above, **Ce may be used in commercial, institutional, or organizational contexts**, provided such use preserves the core principles of the Ce system.

Commercial use is permitted **only when the following conditions are met**:

1. **Agreement remains voluntary**
   Participation in Ce-based systems must not be coerced, enforced through dependency, or conditioned on identity-based control.

2. **Coherence is preserved**
   Implementations must not materially alter or undermine the principles of coherence, agreement, and recall as defined by the Ce protocol.

3. **Ce is not used as a mechanism of control or extraction**
   Ce may not be employed to impose asymmetric power, restrict exit, enforce surveillance, or convert agreement into obligation.

4. **Modifications are disclosed**
   Any material modification or divergence from the Ce protocol must be clearly disclosed.
   Systems that violate these principles may not represent themselves as *Ce*.

5. **Ce itself remains non-owned**
   While Ce may be used within commercial systems, no entity may claim ownership, exclusive control, or proprietary authority over Ce as a system of agreement.

---

Ce is intended to function as **shared infrastructure for coherence and understanding**, not as a proprietary instrument of control.
