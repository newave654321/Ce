# Governance

The Ce protocol is governed through **agreement**, not authority.

---

## Stewardship

The protocol has a steward responsible for:

* Maintaining coherence of the specification
* Reviewing and integrating accepted changes
* Preserving conceptual integrity

Stewardship does not imply ownership of interpretation.

---

## Decision Model

* Changes are proposed openly
* Discussion is recorded
* Acceptance is based on demonstrated coherence and agreement

Identity, status, or external affiliation do not grant decision-making power.

---

## Protocol Authority

All authoritative definitions reside in the `protocol/` directory.

No other documents supersede the protocol.
